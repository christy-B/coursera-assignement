Useful guide for essay writings

Good essay planning

A good essay writing makes the writing easier,makes the path to solution clear,sets your main ideas clearly and relates them to one another.The good essay writingtakes the reader through your answer in a logical and progressive way,helps the reader remember what you have said and groups information in paragraphs.

Model essay structure

It raisesthe reader’s interest,sets the scene,explains how you interpret yourquestions.The introduction defines or explains key terms if necessary and gives a brief outline of which issues you will explore, and in whichorder.
